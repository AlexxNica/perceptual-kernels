How different does a given pair of circles look?

  • We are going to show you 55 pairs of circles and your task is to rate how visually different they look to you!
  • You will enter your rating as a number between 0 and 5 (valid entries: 0, 1, 2, 3, 4, 5). The following picture shows a snapshot of the task interface.
  • A rating of 0 means that there is NO DIFFERENCE between the two circles (they are the SAME).
  • 1 means that the two circles look VERY SIMILAR.
  • 5 means that the two circles look VERY DIFFERENT (for example, 5 times as different as a very similar circle pair).
  • Any rating between 1 and 5 should reflect the degree of visual difference between the circles.
  • Complete each task as accurately and quickly as possible.
  • To get you familiarized with the task interface, we will have a very short training session using squares.

How different does the square pair look to you?

Enter your rating as a number between 0 and 5 by clicking the corresponding radio button or by typing the number directly on the keyboard.
  • A rating of 0 means there is NO DIFFERENCE between the two squares (THE SAME).
  • 1 means the two squares look VERY SIMILAR.
  • 5 means that the two squares look VERY DIFFERENT (for example, 5 times as different as a very similar square pair).
  • Any rating between 1 and 5 should reflect the degree of visual difference between the squares.
